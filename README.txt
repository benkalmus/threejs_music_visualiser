
Running Instructions:

Open a command console in the directory of server.js, then run following command:

node server.js

Open your browser of choice (Chrome/Firefox tested) and server should be hosted at:

localhost:3000


Play:

	Either click anywhere on the page or press "Play" to begin viewing the music visualizer.
	Alternatively, you may upload your own music by clicking on the dashed box, or drag a file to the dashed area.
	Song should autoplay.
